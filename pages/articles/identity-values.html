Identity and Values

SubTitle

I’ve had the privileged to travel to many countries and live in some of them for a while, that gave me a perspective of the world where I could understand the differences in culture, lifestyle, morals and many other interesting things about those places.

Living that experience, I was able to understand and respect all the differences, enjoy and appreciate them, it is incredible how much can you learn from people that are different from you, different beliefs, different language, different culinary, it is amazing how satisfying experiencing those things are, you grow up as a person, you develop more compassion and become more grateful and appreciative for life.

I’ve been living in New Zealand for 5 years, and ever since day one I fell in love for this country, I’ve never felt so welcomed in a foreign country such as here, it is a very multicultural country and everyone is used to living in a such diverse country, I intend to stay here for a long time and help the development of the country and now with software development skills I think I will be able to contribute even more.
