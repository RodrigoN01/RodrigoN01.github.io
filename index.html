Who I am

Front-End Developer based out of Auckland, NZ

Former marketer, self-taught software developer, passionate about technology and web development.

I started my career as a marketer after graduating from business university but found myself spending all of my free time involved with technology, building computers, solving problems, spending hours in front of the computer and being a reference to my friends when it comes to technology. It didn't take long to realize a career change was in order, although it took some time to actually make the move.

Over the years, I have had the privilege of working for big companies such as Walt Disney and Warner Bros where, not only I was able to develope important skills, such as communication and leadership, but also understand the the intricacies of a large corporation, building relationship with clients, helping them build out their brand and become successful.

I began my journey into programing and web development in 2019, when I decided to dedicate full time to my studies and pursue my dreams and do what I am truly passionate about which is web development.
